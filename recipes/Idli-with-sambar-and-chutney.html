Idli Sambar Chutney

Idli, sambar, and chutney unite in a quintessential south indian meal. Soft, steamed idlis soak up the tangy, spiced lentil and vegetable sambar. A vibrant chutney, be it coconut, tomato, or peanut, adds a delightful contrasting flavor and texture, creating a harmonious and satisfying dish.
